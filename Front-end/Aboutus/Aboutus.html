About Game


Welcome to Arm’mo, where we keep track of your meditation history. We understand that a significant challenge faced by many meditators is taining a regular and accurate record of their meditative experiences.

For this reason, we have implemented functional systems that automatically save and display all your meditation data at the click of a button.


In addition to analyzing meditation data, Arm’mo also functions as a meditation guide. To achieve this, we have incorporated the grand meditation principles from "The Mind Illuminated" by Culadasa John, a magnificent book on meditation practice.

About Game
