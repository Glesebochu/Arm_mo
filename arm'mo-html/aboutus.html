Our Dream for Arm’mo Who are We ? Glesebochu is a team of individuals that aim to CREATE the grandest
REALITIES through all of our projects.
A bout US Home We made Arm’mo to aid as a mirror to self, so that a player can see reality
as it truly is by practicing the art of being present. Through this window
we seek to inspire a higher level of consciousness in the hearts of whomever
finds their way to it. It is our wholehearted desire for this game to fuel your
journey towards AWAKENING & beyond.
