For All Your Questions ?

Before Playing the Game

1. How do I create a player account ?
A player account is created as soon as you sign up. To edit your account information you can go to settings -> Account panel & change the information there accordingly.
2. Where do I find information on the stages ?
All stage related information is displayed whenever you select a stage from the home page.
3. Where do I initially save my current stage & where do i get that information ?
When you first sign up you can fill in your current stage. It will also be constantly updated and saved in your Account panel.
4. How do I log out of my account ?
To log out you can go to settings -> log-Out panel and click on the log out button.
5. How do I delete my account ?
To delete your account go to settings -> Delete-Account panel, here you can confirm your action & permanently remove your account.


While Playing the Game

1. How do I choose a meditation activity and meditation object ?
When you start a meditation session the first phase of the game includes filling out your prefered activity & meditation object. You can find these options and more at the start of a session.
2. How do I start a meditation session ?
You can start a new session from the home page, by selecting a stage level which will automaticaly open a new session.
3. How long does a session last ?
A session goes on for as long as you dont click on the end session button.
4. How do I time myself ?
You are timed automatically, the timer goes off when you start a session. You will find all time related information in your session summary.
5. Are there any tips I can follow ?
Tips are offered for every stage befitting the stages challenges & antidotes. You will find these whenever you enter a stage from the home page.
6. Can I move across stages without mastery ?
Yes, you can traverse through stages without mastery. However, your current stage will always be the highest stage you have mastered.
7. How do I traverse through stages ?
You can traverse through stages from the home page or the game console by clicking the next & previous arrow buttons accordingly.
8. How do I know if I mastered a stage ?
At the end of every session there are stage speccific questions that you can answer inorder to master a stage. If you answer according to the guidline you will have mastered a stage and that stage becomes your current stage.

After Playing The Game

1. Where do I get data on the stages I have mastered ?
At the end of every session a session summary is displayed which contains data on all the stages you have mastered during that session.
2. Where do I get my session summary ?
At the end of your session, this means after you click on the 'End-meditaion' button.
3. Are there new features on the way I should know about ?
There is an Arm'mo bot in stock for the next game version, that will better assist you in your meditation journey.
