Introduction
Welcome to Arm’mo!
This is where we keep track of your meditation history. We understand that a significant challenge faced by many meditators is maintaining a regular and accurate record of their meditative experiences. For this reason, we have implemented functional systems that automatically save and display all your meditation data at the click of a button. In addition to analyzing meditation data, Arm’mo also functions as a meditation guide. To achieve this, we have incorporated the grand meditation principles from "The Mind Illuminated" by Culadasa John, a magnificent book on meditation practice.
As a result, the game's user interface (UI) features a very intuitive dialogue sequence that players can undertake to represent their conscious experience and focus their minds. This process applies the preparation and transition phases extensively discussed in the book. Players can also progress through the 10 stages of the road to mastery, with each stage considered mastered based on the player's performance. For more information on these phases and stages, click on the link below.
In simple terms, Arm’mo is a digital ledger and meditation guide that enhances the transformative nature of each of your meditation sessions.
